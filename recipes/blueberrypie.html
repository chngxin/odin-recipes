Blueberry Pie

Blueberry Pie with Frozen Berries

Blueberry pie is the best when made with fresh-picked berries! It is a beautiful sight with a lattice top.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups frozen blueberries
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup salted butter
  • 6 tablespoons cold water
  • 1 tablespoon salted butter
  • ¼ cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Make the filling: Combine frozen blueberries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and salt in a medium pot; mix well to coat blueberries. Cook over medium-high heat, stirring often, 10 to 15 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, make the crust: Combine flour and salt in a large mixing bowl. Cut in 3/4 cup butter with 2 knives or pastry blender until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Add water, 1 tablespoon at a time, tossing with a fork, until flour mixture is moistened.
  4. Divide the dough in half and shape into balls. Roll out 1 dough ball with a rolling pin into a 10-inch circle. Transfer crust to a 9-inch pie dish. Trim the edge of the crust with scissors and crimp the rim with fingertips and knuckles.
  5. Pour filling into prepared pie crust. Dot with 1 tablespoon butter. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and bubbly, about 30 minutes.
